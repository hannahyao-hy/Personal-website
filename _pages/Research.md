My interests lie in end-to-end applied research: defining problems from real-world ambiguity, designing data-driven methods, testing them rigorously, and translating the results into decision-support tools or systems that are useful beyond the experiment.

I am especially interested in LLMs, robotics, multimodal learning, and data science, with applications across finance, capital markets, research workflows, and human-centered products. More broadly, I care about building AI systems that make complex analytical work more explainable, reproducible, and actionable.

## Interests

- LLM, Robotics, Multimodal Learning, Data Science
- Machine learning methods for decision support
- AI applications in finance and capital markets
- Human-centered products
- Efficient tools that make complex analytical work easier to explain and reproduce
